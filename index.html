Our Journey

Our Journey
Begins Here.

Green Petal was born from a desire to bring the raw, untamed beauty of nature into your daily ritual. Every botanical, every clay, every cold-pressed oil is chosen with intention and reverence.

We are not just a brand; we are an atelier. A place where ancient earth minerals meet modern apothecary science to create rituals that nourish the skin and ground the spirit.

01
Small Batch Craft
True alchemy cannot be rushed. We craft our botanical products using traditional methods, ensuring every batch retains its raw nourishing power.
02
Zero Compromise
No synthetic fragrances. No harsh chemicals. Every ingredient is plant-derived, ethically sourced, and chosen for a purpose.
03
10 Years of Mastery
A decade of perfecting formulations, training 2000+ students, and building a community that believes in conscious beauty.
